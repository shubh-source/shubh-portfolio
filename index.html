faq & terms

No hidden surprises.

I believe in 100% transparency. Here is exactly how the money and maintenance works.

Are there any monthly or recurring charges?

The development fee is a one-time cost. However, any external services that require a subscription (like your Domain, Hosting, Backend Servers, Databases, or paid APIs) will be billed to you on actuals. You only pay what those services cost.

What about extra features later?

Anything beyond the initially agreed scope — like adding new pages, complex features, or major design changes after work starts — will be quoted separately before it's built.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Your one-time fee includes basic support for a limited time. If you need ongoing monthly maintenance, backend management, or regular content updates, we can set up a very affordable monthly retainer.
