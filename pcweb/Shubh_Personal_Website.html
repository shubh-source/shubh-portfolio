about

From FMCG distribution to full-stack development.

I'm Shubh Katiyar, from Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh. Before writing a line of code, I spent over eight years in sales and marketing for my family's FMCG bread distribution business, and two years teaching tuition — work that taught me how to sell, negotiate, and read what people actually need, not just what they say they want.

I taught myself to build software because I kept seeing problems that no one in my part of India was building for. HomeDo exists because renting a room in a Tier 2 or 3 city still means paying a broker for information a phone camera could capture. Vlynxly exists because every couples app on the market is either a dating app or built for a market that isn't India.

I have admission secured at IILM University for a B.Tech, but right now HomeDo and Vlynxly are my full-time focus. Everything on this page — the code, the business model, the go-to-market plan — I built myself, without a technical co-founder.
